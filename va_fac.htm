The Art Gallery

The Art Gallery of the Visual Arts Division is a non-profit making gallery committed to the advancement of art education for all the lecturers and students of the HKIEd.

The 500-square foot exhibition space is managed by the Creative Arts Subject Promotion Committee for the following purposes :

  • Exhibitions of work related to art and educational activities
  • Display of students' artwork and portfolios
  • Promotion of artistic and cultural exchanges with local, regional or overseas artists and institutions.

Art Learning & Resource Centre

Conveniently located in the department building and with hours that extend into the evenings and weekends, the CAAS's Art Learning Center effectively meets the specific needs of participants in searching for reference materials and inspiration for their work. The Art Learning Centre staff provides professional assistance to students on request, and is well equipped with facilities for the production of teaching materials from resources. Occasionally participants will enjoy watching documentaries, videos and films on arts topics, or have small group sharing with teaching staff on arts projects. The door of this user friendly place is always wide open for CAAS participants!

Graphic Design Studio

The Graphic Design Studio provides students with a wide-range selection of creative learning options and opportunities. The studio served professional print production, video and audio editing, and multimedia creation. Every student has full access to the Studio for learning and project activities.

Photographic Studio

Our new black and white dark room and studio will start operating in the coming year, the rooms are designed for traditional printing of black and white photographs and simple still and portraiture photography.

Print-making Studio

Full studio facilities and well equipped printmaking workshops are provided for intaglio, lithography, relief and screen printing. Students are encouraged to use which ever is the most appropriate for the realization of their ideas.

Ceramics Studio

The ceramics room provides sufficient applicants for our teachers to demonstrate the skill involved in ceramics and students to develop techniques like form-building, decoration, glazingand firing.
Sculpture Studio

Sculpture Studio, participants are well supported by workshop staff and expert lecturers on manipulation of form, material and realization of creative ideas in their study.

2D Art Studio

two-dimensional studio provides sufficient learning resources and well-equipped facilities for students.
